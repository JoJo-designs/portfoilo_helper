museum exhibit

For this student project I was tasked to create the branding for an museum exhibit. the event would be held at the canadian museum of history. We could choose from a few exhibits that where currently running or would run in the future at the museum. I choose to the neanderthals exhibit not for any particular reason just felt like it.

I wanted the branding ot have organic feeling image. I wanted a cave paining look and used pink-ish orange colours as the main colours then used blue the complement to orange to create a vibrant but also somewhat natural colour scheme.

I looked at some images of real cave painting. I decided that the best way to capture the rough look was to hand draw them and scan them in and image trace.

This poster is used to advertise the event. The majority of the poster is covered with a sorta crude hand drawing meant to look like a cave painting. The title of the event sits near the center in large text. Additional text that would help to explain what the event is about and the date and location is at the bottom of the poster.
Similar to the poster the event booklets cover has the same image and basic layout but without the text explaining the event but instead has the dates and times the the exhibit are open.
The page just inside the cover of the booklet has more general information on the museum. this information incudes stuff like prices, hours of operation and upcoming closers. This page is not specific to the exhibit. I created a grid of differently sized images on the top of the page to add some interest to the design.
The pages about the exhibit have text that was features on the exhibit website. this page has some more cave style drawings of a figure hunting a deer like creature. When I was doing research I learned that neanderthals were actually very intelligent and used complex tools. I used a bubble to separated this information from the rest of the text to help tie the text and images together.
The following page continues the text from the pervious page. I also highlight an interesting fact on this page as well.
This image shows some banners that would be displayed. I wanted to have it so there was several banners that all make up an larger image. The banners depict a stampede of deer running across the banners. the deer are draw in in a cave painting style.
