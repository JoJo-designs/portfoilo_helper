Nintendo Lux Stationery Set

I love playing video games and when I had a chance to create a luxury stationery set for any company I wanted. The choose was easy I did nintendo. When I made this project the nintendo switch had only been out for about a year. This is purely a fan piece and is not real.

Being a luxury stationery set it needed to be super fancy. It can't be seen in these mockups but it would have lots of features like metallic inks and embossing. It would also be printed on a thick card stock.

I had a lot of fun with this project. It was very enjoyable. I was able to put down my love of video games on a school project which was cool. I did make a few edits from the original design.

This layout displays all the part in one picture. The letter head has an image of mario in the bottom corner. This would be embossed and painted in a metallic red ink. on the back of the letter head is a design that features images for some of nintendo's bigger game titles like the legend of zelda, mario and smash bros. (the key chain wasn't made by me and is just the fill the awkward space).
One of the reasons I choose to use nintendo for this project was because I thought the switch unique detachable controllers would make cool business cards (I was right these are really cool). The cards come in a left and right variety matching the game system. The buttons are embossed to add texture and the die cut on the top of the card give the cards the shape of the joy cons.
Inside the envelops have the same patten as the back of the letter head.
