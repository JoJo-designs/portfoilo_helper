Decora Home

The goal of this student project was to create the branding for a high end home and decor store. The store would sell paint, wallpaper and home decor.

Decora is a store that would specialize in home good and paints. The name was inspired by a fashion style of the same name where more contrasting colours and accessories is a must. It is very colourful and fun. The style is overly busy which doesn't really match the final product.

I choose to use round letters for this project. I think this gives it a youthful look but still clean and professional. This is one of my favorite projects that I have worked on.

The logo is on both a dark and light coloured backgrounds. I made the logo with very round and friendly letters. The triangle over the D in the name represents a house.
This simple design was mostly just for my portfolio website. I am using it as the thumbnail image for this project. I liked the way it turned out so I decided to display it alone with everything else. I think it would be good to use as a poster that is hung in the stores front window.
This is a cover for a booklet of colours that would be available. I wanted each booklet to focus on one colour. I found this fantastic image of three different pink balloons on a pink background. It was so pink that I couldn't help myself I had to use it. I remember when I first showed my teacher she asked if I liked pink. I guess she was surprised by the pinkness that she saw before her. I was usually wearing darker colours but I don't mind pink.
When I was researching for this project I found that many companies use well decorated rooms painted in the colour with swatches of colours that are similar or match the colour. This does look nice, however I didn't just want nice rooms in the colour. I wanted to create a focus on one colour and use pictures that are very that colour. So in this case very pink.
I really enjoyed making the booklet so I did it again but this time in blue. I found an image of a jelly fish that screamed blue. This was not originally part of what I handed in. This was something I did after the fact for the fun of it.
The inside of this book is very similar in layout of the other booklet. The design of the booklets is very clean. The top corners have a cut out that parallels the triangle in the logo. this cut out can be found on other minerals as well.
Another part of this assigned was to create paint chips. I looked at real paint chips to understand from other companies to understand what kind of thing I may need to put on them. I made single paint chips with a top corner cut out these are about the size of a business card. I also made colour scheme chip. these have a four colours that look nice together.
